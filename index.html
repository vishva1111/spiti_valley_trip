⚡ Critical Checklist — 5 Boys Car Trip
Must-know before you go
⛽Petrol: Reckong Peo + Kaza = fill full. Chandratal jaata pehla extra 10L jerry can carry karo. Next pump = Manali.
💵Cash: ₹10,000+ per person cash. Reckong Peo ATM = last reliable. Spiti = NO UPI, NO card, cash only.
💊Medicines: Diamox (altitude sickness), Dolo 650, ORS, Disprin, Band-aids, Digene. Start Diamox 1 day before entering altitude.
📱Network: BSNL SIM ek janpas must. Airtel/Jio = no signal in Spiti. Maps.me offline download karo before trip.
🧥Clothes: August = 18-20°C day, 5-10°C night. Thermal, fleece, heavy jacket, raincoat — all carry karo.
🚗Car: Spare tyre must. Basic toolkit + tow rope. 4x4 preferred. Chandratal road = rocky, go slow.
📅Booking NOW: Chitkul (Aug full), Chandratal camps (books 1 month early), Kalpa (limited rooms), Kaza hotels.
💧Water: 2L per person daily minimum. Altitude = dehydration fast. Avoid alcohol first 2 days at high altitude.
